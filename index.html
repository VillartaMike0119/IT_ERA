Connections

Carbon Fiber Material

Carbon fiber is a composite material usually made up of a carbon fiber weaved fabric set in a resin-usually plastic. Carbon fiber helmets are stronger than those made with other commonly used materials. It provides a higher scratch and crack-resistance. It is also lighter than other helmets given their strength, making them easier to wear. It is also perfect for all kinds of weather and are often made with breathable vents to accommodate warm weather riders.

Tinted, Photo chromatic and Reflective Shield

It gives enough tint to tame the sun and can adjust to changes in light levels as conditions change.

Anti Fog Shield

Wearing helmet in cool temperatures or simply passing a foggy area covers the sight of the rider. With the anti-fog shield, fogged view is not a problem anymore.
